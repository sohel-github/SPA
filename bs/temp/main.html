Company

Bloodsoft is specialized in developing web-based services and mobile applications according to the visions of our customers. We are the development partner for efficient and flexible software creation.

Bloodsoft guys and girls are hardcore software professionals, being able to handle complex back-end systems and the latest front-end technologies. In addition to developing web services with mainly open source technologies, we have built a strong mobile application development team during the past years. Knowing the both worlds, web and mobile, has helped us greatly in delivering complete digital solutions for our customers.

Our work atmosphere is relaxed and fun, yet the tasks are challenging enough to keep us learning all the time. We are not back-office nerds, but coders deal with the customers too. We really like what we do and that shows in our work.
